Account Architecture

Mono Protocol pairs account & gas abstraction with smart-account tooling to deliver one-click, cross-chain execution without users touching gas, networks, or bridges.

Features

Account & Gas Abstraction

Smart-contract accounts enable gas sponsorship, batching, and custom auth so users never manage gas or networks.

Credible Smart Accounts

SCA-based accounts can issue Resource Locks to prevent double-spend and unlock Fast Path execution that feels same-chain.

Kernel + Validators 

Kernel 3.1/3.3 with ECDSAValidator and RoleBasedValidator provides flexible, production-grade controls.

Bundlers, Paymasters, WaaS

Infrastructure pieces and embedded wallets streamline signing and gas, reducing dev overhead.
